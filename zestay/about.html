About Us

Who We Are

Zestay is India's first AI-driven roommate matching platform designed to turn the chaotic search for shared living into a seamless, secure, and compatible experience. Born out of the struggle to find "good vibes" in a new city, Zestay connects people not just by budget and location, but by lifestyle, habits, and personality compatibility.

Our Mission

To eliminate the friction of shared living by ensuring every match is based on trust, compatibility, and transparency. We believe your home should be your sanctuary, and the people you share it with make all the difference.
