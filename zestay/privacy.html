Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect personal identifiers (Name, Email, Phone), verification data (Govt ID, Selfie), and behavioral data (Preferences, Activity) to provide our services effectively.

2. Use of Data

IDs are used strictly for identity verification and fraud prevention. They are processed securely and not shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Location Data

We use approximate location data to show you relevant matches in your vicinity. You can disable this in your browser settings at any time.

4. User Visibility

Your phone number is hidden by default and only shared when you explicitly accept a connection request or share it in chat.

5. Data Deletion

You can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing privacy@zestay.com.
