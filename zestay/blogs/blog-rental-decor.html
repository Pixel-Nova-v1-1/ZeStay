Budget-Friendly Ways to Decorate Your Rented PG or Flat

Rented apartments often have extreme clauses regarding wall damage, leaving many renters living inside uninspiring white boxes for years. But you don't need a sledgehammer to make a rental feel like home. Here are damage-free, budget-friendly ways to decorate your space.

1. Lighting is Everything

Ditch the harsh, cold tube lights supplied by the landlord. Invest in warm-toned (yellow) LED floor lamps and fairy lights. Lighting instantly changes the mood of a room from a hospital ward to a cozy sanctuary.

2. Peel-and-Stick Wallpapers

Want an accent wall but can't paint? Removable vinyl wallpaper is a renter's best friend. It sticks securely, looks premium, and peels off flawlessly without leaving any residue or ripping the paint.

3. Command Hooks for Art

Nailing holes means losing your security deposit. Use heavy-duty, damage-free adhesive strips like Command hooks to hang posters, mirrors, and canvases.
