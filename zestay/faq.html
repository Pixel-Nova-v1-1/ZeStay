Frequently Asked Questions

General

Is Zestay completely free?

Yes! Signing up, browsing matches, and chatting with users is currently free for all individual users.

Can I search for flatmates without having a flat?

Absolutely. You can select "Looking for a Flatmate" and team up with others to hunt for a whole flat together ("Team-Up" mode).

Matching & Verification

How is my Match Score calculated?

We use your answers from the "Vibe Check" quiz to compare key compatibility factors like cleanliness, social battery, and daily schedule. A score above 80% usually indicates excellent harmony.

My ID verification failed. Why?

Common reasons include: blurry photos, name mismatch between profile and ID, or an expired document. Please retry with a clear photo.

Listings

How long does my listing stay active?

Listings are active for 30 days by default. You can renew them or mark them as "Occupied" at any time from your dashboard.
